About

Research, Teaching &
Academic Leadership

I am an Associate Professor of Finance at the School of Management, BML Munjal University, Gurugram. My work is driven by a passion for turning data into insights, markets into models, and code into financial foresight.

My research focuses on empirical finance, quantitative methods, and financial econometrics, with particular emphasis on capital markets in emerging economies. I work at the intersection of rigorous statistical modelling and real-world financial phenomena, using tools ranging from panel econometrics to text analysis and machine learning.

I am committed to producing high-impact, policy-relevant research and to training students who can think analytically, work with data, and contribute meaningfully to the finance profession.
